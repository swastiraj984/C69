Places must visit

Prithvi cafe

I had visited this place in Dec 2018

Little restaurant tucked inside a by len of Juhu.Blink and you will miss the entrance to the cafe.So be alert enough to spot the black sign board that says "Prithvi cafe" while you are on the main road.The restaurant has a good menu at resionable price. The food is delicious, the servings are quite large and some dishes can be shared by two people. Attracts quite a trendy crowd of aspiring celebrities on show days.Be sure to book your seats in advance.On other days the crowd is pretty happening.Host the famous Prithvi theatre fest which you must attend if possible



Jantar Mantar-Jaipur

I had visited this place in Feb 2019

Its a wonderful place. Must visit this place.You must hire a tourguide so it will be better for you to understand all the things.It is a beautifully maintained small complex with yellow painted walls.Its a historical site famous among tourist.It contains a number of devices that were used to measure time in different eras in the Indian history.It has a nominal ticket price of Rs 15 for Indians but about 225 to 250 for foreigners.Nice place to visit. We had an incredible experience visiting this observetory and seeing the accuracy of the astronomical instrument buit around 300 years ago.



Yiga Choeling Monastery

I had visited this place in Oct 2019

One of the oldest monastery in Darjeeling.It is situated in peaceful and beautiful landscape just 15 mins walk from Ghoom Railway Station.We can see religious gods and goddesses paintings believed by Budhistsm inside which can give spiritual power to one who prays there with clean heart.From the compound of Monastery on clear day one can see a view of hills all around which is great for nature lovers.
